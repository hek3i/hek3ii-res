Philosophy

Core principles of the Vanta resource atlas.

Vanta acts as an catalog aggregator, keeping tools accessible without tracking or advertising logs.

Open Access

Every script, guide, and utility listed is free and ungatekeeped. There are no registration forms or premium download walls.

Source Integrity

All download nodes display their source domains, checksums, and hosting partners so you know exactly where files are pulled from.

Safety Verification

We encourage checking files via VirusTotal, preferring official repacker communities, and isolating unknown scripts inside secure sandboxes.
